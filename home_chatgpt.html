Life @Marian

Sports

Marian College offers a variety of sports facilities to keep students active and engaged. From basketball courts to a well-maintained football field, we encourage participation in team sports. Indoor activities like table tennis and chess are also available for students who prefer strategic games. Regular sports events and intercollegiate competitions ensure a vibrant athletic culture.

Facilities

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and extracurricular pursuits. The library boasts a vast collection of books and digital resources. We have modern classrooms, laboratories, and IT hubs to foster an environment of learning and innovation. A cafeteria offering diverse cuisines and spacious seminar halls make the campus life enriching.

Hostels

The college provides comfortable and secure hostel accommodations for both boys and girls. Rooms are well-furnished, ensuring a home-away-from-home experience. Each hostel has 24/7 security, Wi-Fi connectivity, and common recreational spaces. Mess facilities serve nutritious meals, catering to a variety of dietary preferences. Events like hostel day foster community and fun.
